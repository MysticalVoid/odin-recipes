Mangu

Mangu with scrambled eggs and onions

Mangu is a dominican dish, it is made of smashed plantains, we have the choice to add some butter or olive oil. You can eat it with cheese, salami(Dominican salami) or eggs.

Ingredients

  • 3 green plantains
  • 1 quart water
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced white onion
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 cup sliced Anaheim peppers
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Peel the plantains, place the plantains and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, and cook 20 minutes, until plantains are tender but slightly firm. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the liquid.

  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion until tender

  3. In a bowl, mash the plantains with the reserved liquid and salt. Transfer to a food processor and puree. Serve the pureed plantain mixture topped with the onions.

  4. In a hot pan, add the eggs, add a pinch of salt and move constantly, when your scrambled eggs are ready, put them with your finished puree.
