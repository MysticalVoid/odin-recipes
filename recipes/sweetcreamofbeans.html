Sweet cream of beans

Sweet cream of beans with vanilla cookies

Sweet cream of beans is a Dominican dessert made with beans, milk, coconut milk, sweet potato, raisins, and spices. The result is a sweet and creamy dessert, similar to a cold soup, that is served with our iconic milk crackers and toasted cassava (a flatbread made from cassava flour).

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds dry red kidney beans
  • 6 (3 inch) cinnamon sticks
  • 2 pounds white sweet potatoes, peeled
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 (12 fluid ounce) cans evaporated milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves, or to taste
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 4 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
